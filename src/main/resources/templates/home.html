Stories of Impact

Hope Foundation

Through Seva Setu, Hope Foundation received donations that helped build 3 new classrooms in rural Maharashtra. Over 150 children now have access to quality education, with 95% showing improved literacy rates within just 6 months.

Impact: 150+ children educated

Green Earth Initiative

With support from our platform, Green Earth Initiative planted 5,000 trees across 10 villages in Karnataka. The project has improved air quality and created sustainable livelihoods for 200 local families through eco-friendly farming practices.

Impact: 5,000 trees planted, 200 families supported

Care & Cure Medical Trust

Through our donation network, Care & Cure Medical Trust provided free medical checkups to 1,000+ people in remote areas of Rajasthan. They also distributed essential medicines and conducted health awareness programs in 15 villages.

Impact: 1,000+ people treated, 15 villages reached
